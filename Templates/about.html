03 : About me

Undergraduate

I graduated from Ohio University in 2019 with a dual degree in Management Information Systems and Business Analytics. In my time in college I was a TA for a MVC Development course, and a Learning Community Leader to assist freshman on the transition to college

Internship

I interned for PNC Bank in their Retail Digital Support Space division. To be quite frank, I was not a fan of the big company atmoshphere. I had felt my skills were overlooked and I did not enjoy the work that I was doing and would be doing had I accepted the full-time position.

Post-Graduate

After graduating, I moved to Columbus, OH to work for SISware as an Implementation Analyst where my job tasks included:

  • Data Cleaning
  • Post-Conversion Fixes

After about 6 months I had been able to show my work ability and was promoted to Lead Conversion Developer where I worked on:

  • Fixing Existing ETL Applications and Processes
  • Client Requests
  • Developing ETL Processes for data-migration of new competitors

After 6 months as the Lead Conversion Developer I had a position created for me as the Lead Product Enhancement Specialist where I:

  • Lead a team of developers in migrating current ETL Processes to database object stored procedures
  • Create dynamic views to clean data once a database has been restored on a server
  • Develop an application to automate the ETL Processes to run with one "click"
  • Develop advanced accounting reports in Exago to integrate with our in-house application
